Our Story

I wanted people to have a taste of what busan food has to offer. Although it's not normalized here, The city of busan is known for its fresh catch from the sea and has a variety of dishes such as raw fish, grilled fish, and seafood stews. In addition to seafood, Busan is also famous for its street food such as hotteok (sweet pancakes), ssiat hotteok (seed-filled pancakes), and milmyeon (cold wheat noodles).
